Design Principles


 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

3 Questions about design principles:

1.What design principle did you choose?

Contrast

2.What have you shown in your poster about these design
principles?

I have made the background-color black and
white to symbolise the contrast design principle. And I have used colourful things to contrast the background.

 

 

 

 

Other Design Principles Include:

Balance

Balance is a distribution of equal visual weight

Alignment

Alignment is an arrangement forming a straight line

Emphasis

Emphasis is an accentuation of importance

Proportion

Proportion is a scaling of objects in relation to each other

Movement

Movement is a directed path of optical motion

Pattern

Pattern is an orderly repetition of an object

Contrast

Contrast is a juxtaposition that accentuates difference

Unity

Unity is a harmonious arrangement of elements
